Web designing is the process of creating and arranging the visual layout, structure, and functionality of websites. It combines creativity with technical skills to produce websites that are attractive, easy to navigate, and responsive across different devices such as computers, tablets, and smartphones. Web designers use tools and technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and design software to develop user-friendly interfaces and engaging experiences. A well-designed website improves user satisfaction, strengthens a brand's online presence, and helps businesses, organizations, and individuals communicate effectively with their audience. As technology continues to evolve, web designing remains an essential skill in the digital world.
